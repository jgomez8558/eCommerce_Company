Find Your Match

About Us

Our Goal

Find Your Match is a booming business dedicated to finding the right candle for you. We at Find Your Match want you to be surrounded in a scent that gives you comofort or have a candle really pops in your home. We offer a broad selection of candles ranging from ordinary candles to specialty candles. Our specialty candles include stress relieving aromatherapy candles, food scented candles, candles shaped after pets, and much more.


Our Story

Find Your Match was founded in McAllen, Texas in 2013. Created by Aylin Lira due to her fascination of fire and with candle's multitude of uses. She then went on to selling her own home made candles down at the local markets in her area. Due to word of mouth of the exemplary level of craftsmanship, her business took off quickly. By 2014, she was already the top candle maker business in her town and had set up a few more shops in Austin, Tx and San Antonio Tx. As of today, Find Your Match is known nationally for their creative style candles as well wide selection of aromatherapy candles.
